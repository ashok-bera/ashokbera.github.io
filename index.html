The dream places

Barcelona

Barcelona is known for it's history , architecture, cuisine,etc. It is located between the mouths of rivers Liobregat and Besos. It is also famous for The Great Football club F.C.Barcelona Known as Catlans.

Paris

Paris is France's capital and it is a global center for art, fashion and culture. And here is the great Eiffel Tower and Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral. And it is also famous for football club P.S.G.

Newyork

New York City comprises 5 boroughs sitting where the Hudson River meets the Atlantic Ocean. Its iconic sites include skyscrapers such as the Empire State Building and sprawling Central Park. Broadway theater is staged in neon-lit Times Square.

New Zeland

New Zeland is an island country in the southwestern Pacific Ocean.The country's varied topography and sharp mountain peaks, including the Southeren alps owe much to tectonic uplift and volcanic eruptions.

Switzerland

Switzerland is a mountainous Central European country, home to numereous lakes, villages and the high peaks of the alps. Its cities contain medieval quarters, with landmarks like capital clock tower and wooden chapel bridge.
